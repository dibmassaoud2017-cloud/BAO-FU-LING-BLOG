## Why Use Camphor Cream for Itchy Skin? The Science Explained

So, what makes camphor a go-to ingredient for itchiness? The magic lies in how it communicates with your nerves.

When you apply camphor to your skin, it activates specific nerve receptors called TRPV1 and TRPA1. These are the same receptors that sense temperature. This interaction creates a distinct cooling or warming sensation that essentially overrides the itch signal being sent to your brain. Your brain gets busy processing the new cooling feeling, and the itch fades into the background. It's a brilliant way to stop the "itch-scratch cycle."

But that's not all. According to health authorities like the [Cleveland Clinic](https://my.clevelandclinic.org/health/drugs/23723-camphor-menthol-cream-gel-or-lotion), camphor also has mild anesthetic and anti-inflammatory properties. This means it doesn't just mask the itch—it actively works to reduce the irritation and inflammation that cause it in the first place.
